The Science, Simply Explained

What is EMS, exactly?

EMS stands for Electrical Muscle Stimulation. It uses low-level electrical impulses — the same kind your own nervous system uses — to gently activate muscles and stimulate the surrounding tissue.

This sends a signal to the body to increase blood flow to the area, which delivers fresh oxygen, removes waste products, and triggers the body's natural recovery response. It's the same technology used by physiotherapists in clinical settings — now available for home use.

The EMSense EMS Massager combines EMS with therapeutic heat and compression, creating a layered approach that clinical research shows is significantly more effective than any single therapy alone.
